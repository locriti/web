Know Who's Connected

We build AI to uniquely recognize individuals. Each user is identified by a unique mix of independent biometric, behavioral, and physical signals. While traditionally considered a security liability, we capitalize on mobile phone ubiquity to sense these signals on demand. For our AI, the sensed data directly evaluates to a single ID, identifying the individual using the device, whether friend, or foe.

When we meet in person, human recognition completes subconsciously. At scale, Locriti continually updates a registry of the people it encounters, allowing you to map from a device to the individual currently using it.

Your Child's Natural Rhythm

Taking care of a baby can sometimes feel like solving a tricky puzzle. To help, Locriti's app works in tandem with our smart anklet to detect when activities like feeding, changing, and playing take place. Locriti then learns your child's routine, and schedules more of these activities in the form of a to-do list. That way, if you're ever unsure of what's next, you can simply advise your list. Learn How

Nap Time for Yourself? Go Right Ahead!

Knowing what's coming lets you plan ahead with certainty. Nap, shower, catch up on missed-calls, get another load of laundry in, or just enjoy a quiet read. We’ve got you covered.

Keep Your Child Happy and Healthy

Infants need good sleep to develop properly. When asked, Locriti will gradually change the timing of items on your list so that following it will help you make sure your little one gets the sleep required to keep growing and stay healthy. Learn How

Live a Little!

Is it date night? Sitters, and even grandparents can have their own lists; simply sync up before you leave, and keep updated when you're away. Have your friends over! Locriti can even tell you if it gets too loud for your little one.
